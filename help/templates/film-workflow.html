Workflow for shooting film

{%endblock%} {% block content %} This guide explains a suggested workflow for shooting film.

Buying film

As soon as you buy a film, add it to Films with information about its type, expiry, cost, etc. It will then be allocated a number and marked as Available for loading into cameras etc.

Loading film

Mark the film as loaded when you load it into the camera. You can also enter a title etc.

Shooting film

Record each exposure you make as a Negative in CameraHub, associated with the film. Each negative will be allocated a unique identifier like #1.5 which consists of the number of the film that the negative belongs to, and the frame number within that film.
